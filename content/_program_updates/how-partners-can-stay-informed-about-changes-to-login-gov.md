To support transparency and help our partner agencies plan for changes, we provide several reliable channels where partners can learn about upcoming updates, technical changes, and service impacts. As a Login.gov partner, you have access to all of them to stay aware of the improvements and updates Login.gov makes regularly to better serve our users.

**[Program updates](https://www.login.gov/partners/program-updates/)** on Login.gov highlight major milestones, new capabilities, policy changes, and roadmap developments. Partners can also subscribe to our monthly partner newsletter, which shares important announcements, reminders, upcoming events, and feature updates in one consolidated summary.

For real-time operational awareness, partners should monitor **[status.login.gov](http://status.login.gov)**, which provides live system status, incident updates, and scheduled maintenance windows. 

Technical teams can stay informed through multiple developer-focused resources. The **[Login.gov developer site](http://developers.login.gov)** ([developers.login.gov](http://developers.login.gov)) is regularly updated with integration guidance, configuration details, and implementation notes. For more in-depth technical changes, the delivery team publishes [release notes in GitHub](https://github.com/18F/identity-idp/releases), documenting enhancements, bug fixes, and technical updates that may impact integrations.

Partners can also engage directly with the Login.gov team. Agencies may submit support requests through Zendesk via the [Support page](https://developers.login.gov/support/#contacting-partner-support), where we provide integration assistance and troubleshooting. Many partners also collaborate with us in a shared Slack channel, #login-partner-support. You can request access to this channel by opening a Zendesk ticket. This channel includes other partners and Login.gov engineers that occasionally chime in. Additionally, the [Login.gov Help Center (login.gov/help)](http://login.gov/help) provides step-by-step guidance and FAQs that agencies can share directly with their users.

By leveraging these channels,  partners can remain informed about ongoing improvements across Login.gov. If you have any questions about accessing them, please contact [partners@login.gov](mailto:partners@login.gov).
